About the Owner

Death By Sugar is owned and operated by Kira Bernard in her home kitchen. Kira is a Registered Nurse at a local children's hospital and operates Death By Sugar as a passion project. A self-described Hufflepuff, she began baking for coworkers as a way to increase morale and decrease the stress that comes with working in healthcare.

From an early age, Kira has identified with goth subculture and enjoys creating cakes with spooky motifs. She views baking as an artistic outlet and a means of spreading joy to others.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of events do you make cakes for?

    All kinds - Birthday parties, baby showers, holidays, anniversaries - you name it, I'll bake for it (except maybe your pro-Trump fundraiser)

  • What kind of food allergies can you accommodate?

    I'm always happy to accommodate any and all special diets. Because I use the same equipment for all my projects, please know that I can't guarantee zero contact with allergens.

  • Can you make cupcakes?

    Yep!

  • How much advance notice do you need before an event?

    Please submit your order request at least one week before the event date. If you can submit it earlier than that, that's even better.

  • Can you make me a normal cake or does it have to be creepy?

    I make cakes of all kinds! The look and feel of the cake is up to you. But spooky cakes are my specialty.

    • I have an [prop or decoration] that I want on my cake - can you incorporate it?

    It's hard to say without knowing what the item is, but I'm happy to try.

  • Can you use black icing?

    Deep black icing looks incredible but usually doesn't taste great. For that reason, I try to use as little food dye as possible, but I'm always experimenting with tastier ways to get the darkest possible icing.

  • How much will it cost?

    Because time, complexity, size of the cake, and ingredients needed vary so widely, it's pretty difficult to say. I'll be sure to work with you in order to reach a number you and I are both comfortable with.

  • Where can I follow you on social media?

    Death By Sugar doesn't yet have its own social media account, but you're welcome to follow my personal Instagram if you don't mind tons of cat photos and occasional photos of roadkill: @kirabernard

  • Where can I ask more questions?

    Feel free to drop any questions or comments here: kira.bernard498@gmail.com.
